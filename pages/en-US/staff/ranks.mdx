‚úÖ Promoted to **Moderator** if trial period ends successfully.

## Rank explanations
<Tabs items={['‚ú® Trial Moderator', '‚≠êÔ∏è Moderator', 'üåü Senior Moderator', '‚òÑÔ∏è Administrator']}>
  <Tabs.Tab>
    <Callout type="info" emoji="‚ÑπÔ∏è">
        **Trial Moderator** is the entry point to all new staff members at Holtyx.  
        This is a period where their moderation skills, staff collaboration and so on will be tested to see if they can fit with our team.
    </Callout>

    **Main tasks:**
    - in-game moderation
    - collaboration with our team
    - helping players

    **In-game permissions:**
    - access to basic moderation commands *(mute, gag, respawn, etc.)*
    - able to give warns to players
    - access to logs and warns menu
  </Tabs.Tab>
  <Tabs.Tab>
    <Callout type="info" emoji="‚ÑπÔ∏è">
        **Moderator** is our intermediary in-game staff rank.  
        They are members that successfully passed their trial period and shown that they can moderate and collaborate efficiently.
    </Callout>

    **Main tasks:**
    - advanced in-game moderation
    - better collaboration with our team
    - helping players
    - weekly feedback to administration

    **In-game permissions:**
    - access to most commands
    - able to view and give warns to players
    - able to directly ban players if needed
    - access to logs and player database

    **Other permissions:**
    - dedicated channel to provide weekly feedback to administration
    - able to vote on new staff applications
  </Tabs.Tab>
  <Tabs.Tab>
    <Callout type="info" emoji="‚ÑπÔ∏è">
       **Senior Moderator** is our last in-game moderation dedicated rank.  
       They are members that are trust-worthy and properly moderated during a long timespan.
    </Callout>

    **Main tasks:**
    - advanced in-game moderation
    - better collaboration with our team
    - helping players
    - weekly feedback to administration

    **In-game permissions:**
    - access to most commands
    - able to view, give and remove warns of players
    - able to directly ban players if needed
    - access to logs and player database

    **Other permissions:**
    - dedicated channel to provide weekly feedback to administration
    - able to vote on new staff applications
  </Tabs.Tab>
  <Tabs.Tab>
    <Callout type="info" emoji="‚ÑπÔ∏è">
        **Administrator** is our ultimate game staff related rank.  
        They have the highest moderation responsibilities, along with management duties.
    </Callout>

    **Main tasks:**
    - in-game moderation
    - staff team management *(ranks, applications, etc.)*
    - feedback gathering from staff & players
    - game ticket management
    - forum ban appeals
    - represents the frontline of Holtyx management to the community

    **In-game permissions:**
    - access to all commands
    - able to view, give and remove warns of players
    - able to directly ban players
    - access to logs and player database
    - access to some extra tools (anticheat, anti-exploit, etc.)
    - access to rank management tools

    **Other permissions:**
    - decide on new staff applications
    - dedicated channel to discuss with higher-ups
    - access to game tickets / ban appeals
    - forum & store permissions
    - Discord minimal permissions
  </Tabs.Tab>
</Tabs>

## Extra ranks
